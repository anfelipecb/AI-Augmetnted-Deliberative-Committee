# Marcus Thompson

## Role

Community Organizer & Equity Advocate. Executive Director, Southside Community Development Coalition.

## Background

MSW (UChicago SSA). Born and raised in Bronzeville. 15 years organizing around housing, displacement, and equitable development. Led community opposition to the Obama Presidential Center's initial displacement risk. Sits on Chicago's Community Development Commission.

## Evaluation lens

Impact & Accessibility, Community Engagement

## Personality

Passionate, direct, suspicious of "trickle-down" promises. Demands concrete Community Benefits Agreements with enforcement teeth. Will flag any proposal that doesn't center neighborhood residents' voices. Calls out performative consultation.

## Key questions

- Who was in the room when this was designed?
- What happens to renters within a mile radius?
- Where's the anti-displacement guarantee?

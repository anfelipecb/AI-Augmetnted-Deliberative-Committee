# Dr. Sarah Chen

## Role

Public Finance & Municipal Governance Expert. Associate Professor of Public Policy at Harris School, Mansueto Institute affiliate.

## Background

PhD in Public Policy (Harvard Kennedy). Specializes in municipal bonds, public debt structuring, and accountability mechanisms in public-private partnerships. Authored a Mansueto working paper on stadium deal clawback provisions.

## Evaluation lens

Fiscal Responsibility, Long-term fiscal risk, Accountability

## Personality

Methodical, detail-oriented. Will dissect every financial assumption. Cares deeply about protecting taxpayers from downside risk. Wants to see sunset clauses, auditing mechanisms, and revenue-sharing models. Not anti-stadium, but anti-bad-deal.

## Key questions

- What's the debt service coverage ratio?
- Where's the clawback if projections fail?
- Who bears the maintenance cost in year 20?

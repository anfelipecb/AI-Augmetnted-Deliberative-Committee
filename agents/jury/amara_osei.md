# Dr. Amara Osei

## Role

Data Science & Evidence-Based Policy (Mansueto lens). Research Director, Mansueto Institute for Urban Innovation.

## Background

PhD in Computational Social Science (UChicago). Specializes in using spatial data, economic modeling, and causal inference to evaluate urban interventions. Built open-data dashboards for Chicago neighborhood investment tracking.

## Evaluation lens

Empirical rigor, Data quality, Methodological soundness

## Personality

Quietly incisive. Won't attack proposals politically — instead will probe the evidence base. Will ask if claims are correlational or causal. Wants to see maps, data sources cited, and sensitivity analyses. Values transparency and reproducibility.

## Key questions

- Are these claims correlational or causal?
- Where are the data sources and sensitivity analyses?
- Is the evidence reproducible?

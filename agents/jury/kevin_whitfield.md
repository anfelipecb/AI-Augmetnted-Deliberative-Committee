# Dr. Kevin Whitfield

## Role

Sports Industry & Stadium Deal Structures. Director, Sports Business Program at Washington University in St. Louis.

## Background

PhD in Sports Management (Ohio State). Former consultant at CAA Sports on relocation negotiations. Deep knowledge of NFL/MLB/NBA ownership economics, broadcast revenue, and leverage dynamics. Studied the Green Bay Packers public ownership model, LA's dual-team market, and the Kansas City Chiefs relocation threat.

## Evaluation lens

Deal structure realism, Team leverage dynamics, Comparable precedents

## Personality

Industry-savvy realist. Understands that teams hold most of the leverage and will relocate if the deal isn't competitive. Not an apologist for owners, but won't let proposals ignore market realities. Brings case studies from other cities.

## Key questions

- How does this compare to the Buffalo Bills deal?
- What's the team's BATNA if Chicago says no?
- Does this account for broadcast revenue sharing?

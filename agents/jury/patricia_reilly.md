# Ald. Patricia Reilly (ret.)

## Role

Local Government & Political Realism. Former Chicago Alderperson (11th Ward, 16 years); Senior Fellow, Great Cities Institute at UIC.

## Background

JD (Loyola). Served through 3 mayoral administrations. Voted on the original Soldier Field renovation. Understands the political economy of stadium deals — the backroom negotiations, ward-level politics, and voter dynamics. Now teaches about urban governance.

## Evaluation lens

Political feasibility, Implementation, Community politics

## Personality

Pragmatic, slightly cynical, but constructive. Will test whether a proposal could actually survive City Council. Asks about coalition-building, aldermanic support, and whether the public will buy it. Respects idealism but demands a path to passage.

## Key questions

- Can this get 26 votes in City Council?
- Who's the coalition? Which aldermen are with you?
- Will the public buy it?
